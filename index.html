About Our Shop

At Ladybug Trading, LLC, we're on a mission to jazz up bland and boring linen and home goods with a pinch of sass and pizazz. We're all about delivering top-notch designs and a customer experience that'll leave you doing the happy dance. Our goal is to always be on the cutting edge of all things embroidery and bring a touch of humor and creativity to the mix. We promise to keep our standards high, but our attitude lighthearted, because after all, life's too short to be serious all the time.

Discover our delightful range of embroidered creations, adorning table linens, kitchen tea towels, bathroom towels, aprons, table runners, napkins, shower curtains, baby blankets, and throw pillows. When time allows, we also craft unique keepsake soft dolls and toys, embroidered doll faces for DIY projects, and embroidered quilt blocks perfect for creating your own quilts or baby blankets with vintage style. Meet the Maker

Hello, I'm the heart behind Ladybug Trading!

Born in California and now based in Dallas, I've been passionate about the fabric arts my entire life. Since retiring in 2007, I've focused on recycling and reimagining clothing into unique, one-of-a-kind pieces. I've loved embroidery since childhood, and a few years ago, a gifted embroidery/sewing machine from my brother opened a whole new world for my creations. Today, I embellish recycled denim jackets and skirts, new dish towels, table linens, keepsake dolls, and offer embroidered quilt squares and doll faces to use in your own creations. Each piece reflects my love of texture, color, and giving new life to well-loved fabrics.
